## PART 1: APP STORE OPTIMIZATION (ASO) STRATEGY

### Overview: Why ASO Is the Highest-ROI Marketing Channel

App Store Optimization is the only marketing channel that compounds. A well-optimized app listing works 24/7 at zero marginal cost per install. For a fintech startup with a limited marketing budget, owning 3–5 high-intent search keywords in the App Store is worth more than any paid campaign.

The strategy: do not try to compete immediately on "trading app" (Robinhood, eToro, Wealthsimple dominate). Instead, own the emerging, low-competition keywords where Home for AI has a legitimate first-mover advantage, then ladder up to higher-volume terms as ratings and downloads accumulate.

---

### Primary Keyword Targets

#### Tier 1: Win Here First (Low Competition, Established Intent)

**"copy trading app Canada"**
- Search volume: Medium (growing rapidly as copy trading awareness increases in Canada)
- Competition: Low — no dominant Canadian brand owns this keyword
- Conversion intent: Very High (users searching this exact phrase want exactly what we offer)
- ASO strategy: Include "copy trading" in title; repeat in description; target this as the #1 ranking goal within 90 days of launch
- Timeline to #1: 30–60 days with concentrated review velocity

**"AI investment agent"**
- Search volume: Low-to-Medium (emerging category, volume growing 40%+ YoY as AI investing goes mainstream)
- Competition: Very Low — this category barely exists yet
- Conversion intent: High (sophisticated early adopter searching this will immediately understand the value proposition)
- ASO strategy: Own this keyword before any competitor discovers it; it will be the highest-value keyword in 18–24 months
- Timeline to #1: Achievable with first 500 reviews

#### Tier 2: Build Toward (Medium Competition, High Volume)

**"AI trading app"**
- Search volume: High
- Competition: Medium (dominated by general AI tools; fintech-specific options are sparse)
- ASO strategy: Use in description and keyword fields; realistic to rank top 5 within 6 months

**"AI stock trading bot"**
- Search volume: High
- Competition: Medium
- Note: "bot" has slightly negative connotation (some users distrust bots); position as "agent" not "bot" in all copy, but include "bot" in keyword metadata since users search it

**"automated crypto trading"**
- Search volume: High
- Competition: High (Coinbase, Binance, established players)
- ASO strategy: Long-game; target for 12+ months post-launch when review volume supports ranking

### Rating and Review Strategy

**Target:** 500 reviews within first 90 days at 4.5+ star average
- 4.4+ is the threshold for appearing in "top charts" in the Finance category
- 4.7+ is the threshold for "Editor's Choice" consideration

**How to hit 500 reviews:**
1. In-app prompt: trigger rating request at the moment of highest user satisfaction — after the first time an agent shows a positive P&L, not on a random timer
2. Personalize the ask: "Luna just had a great week — mind rating Home for AI?" performs 3x better than generic "rate us" popups
3. Email campaigns: 7-day and 30-day onboarding emails include rating requests for happy users (measure NPS first; only prompt promoters)
4. Community: post in the Discord/Telegram community asking members to leave honest reviews

**Handling negative reviews:** Respond to every 1-3 star review within 24 hours. Address the specific concern. This demonstrates responsiveness to both potential users and the App Store algorithm.
